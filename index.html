Cloudiness

Humidity

Seems there is no relationship between the latitude and humidity. Varieties of measurements can still be found regardless of latitude .

Max temperature

Correlations can be found between latitude and temperature. 0 Represents the equator, and the closer you approach it the warmer it gets. Weather peaks at -20 Degrees Latitude, rather than at 0 Degrees Latitude, which may be due to obliquity, which is the angle of the planets tilt

Wind Speed

Seems wind speed is usually around 0-15 mph regardless of latitude, with some exceptions beyond those measurements. Highest speed recorded is around 35 mph
